Polymarket Analytics

Polymarket Trading System — automated prediction market analysis pipeline. Collects trade & orderbook data, computes technical indicators (RSI, MACD, volatility), generates ML signals (probability of price increase), and runs backtests. Price on a prediction market = probability of the outcome: 0.80 means 80% chance of "Yes".

RSI (14-period)

Relative Strength Index. Above 70 = overbought (price may drop). Below 30 = oversold (price may rise). Between 30–70 = neutral.

MACD

When MACD line crosses above the signal line = bullish trend. Below = bearish. Histogram bars show trend strength.

Feature Correlation Matrix

How strongly features are related to each other. Green = positive correlation, red = negative. Strong correlations (|r| > 0.5) may indicate redundant features.

What is a signal? The ML model analyzes current market features and outputs the probability of price increase (0–1). Value ≥ 0.55 = Buy recommendation, < 0.35 = Sell, 0.35–0.55 = Hold (uncertain).

Signal Distribution

How many signals fell into each probability bucket. Green = buy zone, red = sell zone, amber = hold.

Average predicted probability per market. Green > 0.6 (bullish), red < 0.4 (bearish), amber = neutral.
